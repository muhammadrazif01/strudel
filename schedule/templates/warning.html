Operation Declined!


Here are things you have to know regarding ReSeat operation!
  • Creating a new timeslot
    • Finish Time can't be earlier than the Start Time
    Total Seat and Available Seat input can't be a negative number
    Value for 'Seat Availability' can't exceed the 'Total Seat'
  • Updating a timeslot
    • The new 'Total Seat' can't be negative! There should at least be one seat
  • Deleting a timeslot
    • You can only delete a timeslot if there is none reservation has been made on that timeslot
